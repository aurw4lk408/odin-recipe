Guacamole

Description

With the Superbowl coming up soon, now's a great time to learn how to make some guacamole! It's a great appetizer that everyone enjoys, and is easy to make.

Ingredients

  • Avocadoes
  • Lime
  • Salt
  • Onion and roman tomatoes
  • Cilantro, galic, and cayenne pepper

Steps

  1. Mash avocadoes, lime juice, and salt together. Mix in tomatoes, onion, cilantro, and carlic. Stir in cayenne pepper
  2. Serve immediately, or cover and refrigerate for 1 hour for improved flavor.
