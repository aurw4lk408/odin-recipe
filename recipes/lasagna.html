Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a baked italian dish made up of pasta that is typically layered with meat, cheese, and tomato sauce. It is a delicious meal that surprisingly is easy to make!

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion and garlic
  • Tomato sauce
  • Sugar
  • Spices and seasonings
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheeses
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the ricotta mixture
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the recipe instructions
  5. Cover with foil and bake
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving.
